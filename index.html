About Queens Of Change Foundation

Queens Of Change Foundation is a women-led NGO dedicated to transforming the lives of underprivileged women and girls across India. We believe that menstrual health should never be a barrier to dignity, education, or opportunity.

Founded on the principle that every woman deserves respect and support, we work directly in communities to provide sanitary pads, health education, and empowerment opportunities. We are 100% women-led and driven by the lived experiences of the communities we serve.

Our approach is holistic — we don't just distribute products, we transform beliefs, break stigma, and create pathways to independence for women and girls.

Our Mission, Vision & Approach

The values that guide everything we do

🌸

Mission

To restore dignity, equality, and opportunity for women and girls through hygiene access, health awareness, and practical skills that create real, lasting change.

✨

Vision

Our 3 Main Programs

Simple programs with real, measurable impact

🌿

Sanitary Pad Distribution

Thousands of girls miss school every month because they cannot afford sanitary pads. We distribute free hygiene kits so no girl has to choose between her period and her education. Every kit includes pads, hygiene supplies, and basic guidance.

💡

Health & Menstrual Education

Menstruation is still surrounded by shame and misinformation in many communities. We visit schools and neighbourhood groups to run open, honest sessions that break taboos and give girls and women the knowledge they deserve.

💼

Women's Skills Development

We run vocational training and confidence workshops for women who want to earn and become independent. Women in our programs have gone on to start their own small businesses and support their families with pride.
